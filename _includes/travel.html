Getting Here

Closest airports:

Dundee

Edinburgh


From Dundee:

  • Cheapest: bus to St. Andrews (service 99)
  • Bus or taxi to train station ->Train to leuchars + bus to St. Andrews (service 99)
  • Car rental
  • Taxi from airport ~£30 to St. Andrews


From Edinburgh

  • St Andrews Direct and St Andrews Shuttle leaves from the airport (book in advance)

  • Bus+train+bus
    1. Airport Airlink bus (service 100) ~30min to Waverley Bridge ~£4.50
    2. Waverly Station is situation by Waverley Bridge, take a train to Dundee, Aberdeen or Dyce
    3. Get off at Leuchars station
    4. Take the bus (service 99) to St Andrews or take a taxi

  • Tram+train+bus
    1. Tram to Haymarket station
    2. Take train from Haymarket station going towards Dundee, Aberdeen or Dyce
    3. Get off at Leuchars station
    4. Take the bus (service 99) to St Andrews or take a taxi

  • Taxi ~£80 from airport to St. Andrews
  • Car rental from Edinburgh


From London

  • Fly to Edinburgh or Dundee, follow above instructions
    • If going to Edinburgh, change trains at Waverley station or Haymarket station, for a train going to Dundee, Aberdeen or Dyce, get off at Leuchars, and take bus line 99 or a taxi to St. Andrews

  • Train from Kings Cross station going to Edinburgh or Aberdeen
    • If on a train from London going to Aberdeen, get off at Leuchars station and take bus line 99 or taxi to St. Andrews
